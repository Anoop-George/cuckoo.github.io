Website development

Main things to consider, this article assumes that reader is new to website development

Static websites

They are pretty much less dependant with backend service, I mean it have client facing side only They may not perform complex functions but it can act as a information provider to client. The coding may be done with HTML,CSS and Javascript

Dynamic websites

They are heavily dependant on the backend. The frontend is usually designed in HTML,JS and CSS. The backend is with PHP, Python or any language. Dynamic websites may have complex functions , ranging from e commerce to ERP.
